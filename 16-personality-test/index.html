Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the test take?

The test takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. It consists of 40 questions that assess your preferences across four key dimensions: Extraversion/Introversion, Sensing/Intuition, Thinking/Feeling, and Judging/Perceiving.

Is the test free?

Yes, the test is completely free. You can take it as many times as you like without any registration or payment required.

How accurate are the results?

The results are based on your honest responses and provide insights into your personality preferences. While not a clinical diagnosis, the test offers valuable self-understanding and can help guide personal and professional development.

What personality types are there?

There are 16 personality types, including INTJ (Architect), ENFP (Campaigner), INFJ (Advocate), and others. Each type has unique characteristics, strengths, and career paths.
