Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Enneagram test take?

The test takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. It consists of questions designed to identify your core motivations and personality patterns.

Is the Enneagram test free?

Yes, the test is completely free. You can take it as many times as you like without any registration or payment required.

How is Enneagram different from MBTI?

While MBTI focuses on how you process information and make decisions, the Enneagram focuses on why you behave the way you do - your core motivations and fears. Both systems offer valuable but different insights.

Can I be more than one Enneagram type?

You have one primary Enneagram type, but you may relate to aspects of other types. The Enneagram also describes "wings" (adjacent types) and "stress/growth" points that influence your behavior.

What if I'm not sure about my type?

It's common to relate to multiple types initially. Read descriptions of the types you're considering, and think about your core motivation and fear. The type that resonates most deeply with your inner experience is likely your type.
