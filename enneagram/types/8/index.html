Enneagram Type 8 - The Challenger

Core Motivation: To be self-reliant and in control

Type 8 Overview

Type 8s are self-confident, decisive, willful, and confrontational. They are driven to be independent and protect themselves and others.

Core Motivation: To be self-reliant and in control

Core Fear: Being controlled or vulnerable

Type 8 Strengths

  • Strong and assertive
  • Natural leaders
  • Protective and loyal
  • Direct and honest
  • Excellent at taking action

Type 8 Weaknesses

  • May struggle with vulnerability
  • Can be controlling or domineering
  • May have difficulty with softness or gentleness
  • Can be confrontational or aggressive
  • May struggle with admitting weakness

Growth Tips for Type 8

  • Practice vulnerability and emotional expression
  • Learn to soften and be gentle with others
  • Develop patience and tolerance
  • Allow others to take the lead sometimes
  • Connect with your softer, more vulnerable side
