Enneagram Type 1 - The Perfectionist

Core Motivation: To be good, right, and perfect

Type 1 Overview

Type 1s are principled, purposeful, self-controlled, and perfectionistic. They strive to improve themselves and the world around them.

Core Motivation: To be good, right, and perfect

Core Fear: Being corrupt, defective, or evil

Type 1 Strengths

  • High standards and integrity
  • Strong sense of right and wrong
  • Reliable and responsible
  • Detail-oriented and thorough
  • Self-disciplined and organized

Type 1 Weaknesses

  • Perfectionism can lead to procrastination
  • Critical of themselves and others
  • May struggle with flexibility
  • Can be overly self-critical
  • May have difficulty relaxing

Growth Tips for Type 1

  • Practice self-compassion and accept imperfection
  • Learn to distinguish between what is essential and what is not
  • Allow yourself to make mistakes and learn from them
  • Practice flexibility and adaptability
  • Balance work with rest and relaxation
