Enneagram Type 4 - The Individualist

Core Motivation: To find themselves and their significance

Type 4 Overview

Type 4s are self-aware, sensitive, reserved, and emotionally honest. They are driven to express their individuality and find meaning.

Core Motivation: To find themselves and their significance

Core Fear: Having no identity or personal significance

Type 4 Strengths

  • Creative and artistic
  • Emotionally authentic and deep
  • Intuitive and insightful
  • Appreciate beauty and aesthetics
  • Compassionate and empathetic

Type 4 Weaknesses

  • May struggle with envy and comparison
  • Can become moody or melancholic
  • May feel misunderstood
  • Can be overly self-absorbed
  • May struggle with self-esteem

Growth Tips for Type 4

  • Practice gratitude and focus on what you have
  • Engage in regular creative expression
  • Build healthy routines and structure
  • Connect with others who understand you
  • Accept both positive and negative emotions
