What is MBTI?

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a personality assessment tool that categorizes individuals into 16 distinct personality types based on four key dimensions:

  • Extraversion (E) vs Introversion (I): How you gain energy and focus your attention
  • Sensing (S) vs Intuition (N): How you perceive and gather information
  • Thinking (T) vs Feeling (F): How you make decisions
  • Judging (J) vs Perceiving (P): How you approach the outside world
