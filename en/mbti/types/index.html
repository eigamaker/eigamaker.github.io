16 MBTI Personality Types

Complete guide to all personality types

The MBTI framework categorizes personalities into 16 distinct types based on four dimensions. Click on any type below to learn more about its characteristics, strengths, weaknesses, and career matches.

INTJ - Architect

Strategic, independent, and analytical. Visionary planners who excel at long-term thinking.

INTP - Logician

Curious and independent thinkers who love exploring complex ideas.

ENTJ - Commander

Strategic leaders who are decisive and goal-oriented.

ENTP - Debater

Innovative and analytical, ENTPs enjoy challenging assumptions.

INFJ - Advocate

Insightful and purpose-driven, INFJs combine empathy with vision.

INFP - Mediator

Idealistic and empathetic, guided by inner values and meaning.

ENFJ - Protagonist

Warm and organized, ENFJs motivate people toward shared goals.

ENFP - Campaigner

Imaginative and people-focused, ENFPs spot possibilities quickly.

ISTJ - Logistician

Responsible and methodical, ISTJs value rules and reliability.

ISFJ - Defender

Loyal and attentive, ISFJs protect stability and support people.

ESTJ - Executive

Structured and dependable, ESTJs focus on efficiency.

ESFJ - Consul

Practical and caring, ESFJs look after people and keep things running.

ISTP - Virtuoso

Hands-on and adaptable, ISTPs like figuring out how things work.

ISFP - Adventurer

Gentle and flexible, ISFPs value beauty and authenticity.

ESTP - Entrepreneur

Action-oriented and pragmatic, ESTPs solve problems quickly.

ESFP - Entertainer

Energetic and spontaneous, ESFPs live in the present.
