Enneagram Type 3 - The Achiever

Core Motivation: To be valuable and worthwhile

Type 3 Overview

Type 3s are self-assured, attractive, charming, and ambitious. They are driven to succeed and be the best version of themselves.

Core Motivation: To be valuable and worthwhile

Core Fear: Being worthless or without value

Type 3 Strengths

  • Goal-oriented and driven
  • Adaptable and efficient
  • Confident and charismatic
  • Excellent at presenting themselves
  • Highly productive and achievement-focused

Type 3 Weaknesses

  • May prioritize image over authenticity
  • Can become workaholic
  • May struggle with vulnerability
  • Can be overly competitive
  • May lose touch with their true feelings

Growth Tips for Type 3

  • Connect with your authentic self and values
  • Allow yourself to be vulnerable with others
  • Balance achievement with rest and relationships
  • Practice self-reflection and emotional awareness
  • Value yourself beyond achievements
