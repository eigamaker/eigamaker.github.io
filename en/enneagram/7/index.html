Enneagram Type 7 - The Enthusiast

Core Motivation: To maintain happiness and satisfaction

Type 7 Overview

Type 7s are spontaneous, versatile, acquisitive, and scattered. They are driven to experience life fully and avoid pain.

Core Motivation: To maintain happiness and satisfaction

Core Fear: Being trapped in pain or deprivation

Type 7 Strengths

  • Enthusiastic and optimistic
  • Spontaneous and adventurous
  • Creative and innovative
  • Excellent at generating ideas
  • Bring energy and joy to others

Type 7 Weaknesses

  • May avoid difficult emotions or pain
  • Can become scattered or unfocused
  • May struggle with commitment
  • Can be impulsive or restless
  • May have difficulty with limits or boundaries

Growth Tips for Type 7

  • Practice staying present with difficult emotions
  • Focus on completing projects before starting new ones
  • Develop depth and commitment
  • Learn to appreciate what you have
  • Balance excitement with stability
