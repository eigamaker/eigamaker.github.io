Enneagram Type 6 - The Loyalist

Core Motivation: To have security and support

Type 6 Overview

Type 6s are engaging, responsible, anxious, and suspicious. They are driven to find security and support in relationships and systems.

Core Motivation: To have security and support

Core Fear: Being without support or guidance

Type 6 Strengths

  • Loyal and committed
  • Responsible and reliable
  • Excellent at anticipating problems
  • Courageous when facing challenges
  • Strong sense of duty and responsibility

Type 6 Weaknesses

  • May struggle with anxiety and doubt
  • Can be overly cautious or suspicious
  • May seek external authority for guidance
  • Can become defensive or reactive
  • May struggle with indecision

Growth Tips for Type 6

  • Develop trust in yourself and your own judgment
  • Practice facing fears directly
  • Build self-confidence and inner security
  • Learn to distinguish between real and imagined threats
  • Cultivate inner authority and guidance
