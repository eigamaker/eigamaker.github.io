Enneagram Type 2 - The Helper

Core Motivation: To be loved and needed

Type 2 Overview

Type 2s are caring, interpersonal, demonstrative, and generous. They are motivated by a desire to be loved and to help others.

Core Motivation: To be loved and needed

Core Fear: Being unwanted or unworthy of love

Type 2 Strengths

  • Empathetic and caring
  • Excellent at reading others' emotions
  • Generous and giving
  • Warm and supportive
  • Strong interpersonal skills

Type 2 Weaknesses

  • May neglect their own needs
  • Can become overly dependent on others' approval
  • May struggle with setting boundaries
  • Can be manipulative when needs aren't met
  • May have difficulty receiving help

Growth Tips for Type 2

  • Learn to recognize and express your own needs
  • Set healthy boundaries with others
  • Practice self-care regularly
  • Allow yourself to receive help and support
  • Develop independence and self-reliance
